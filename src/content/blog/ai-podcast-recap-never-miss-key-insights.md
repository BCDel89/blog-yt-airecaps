**Does AI Recaps work with all podcasts, or only certain ones?**

AI Recaps currently works with podcasts and videos published to YouTube. Since most major podcasts now maintain a YouTube channel alongside their audio feed, this covers the majority of popular shows. If your favorite podcast publishes to YouTube, you can recap it.

**How accurate are AI podcast recaps compared to the real episode?**

AI recaps are generated from the actual transcript of the episode — not from a description or metadata. Accuracy is high for factual content, specific quotes, and key arguments. The AI summarizes what was actually said, so you're not getting a paraphrase of a paraphrase. As with any AI tool, it's worth verifying specific claims before citing them professionally.

**Can I use AI Recaps for episodes in languages other than English?**

YouTube generates transcripts in many languages, and AI Recaps can process transcripts across supported languages. English-language content currently has the best transcript quality, but the platform works with other languages where accurate transcripts are available.
